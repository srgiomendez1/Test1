## The 5 Non-Negotiables (Start Here)

1. **Take methylfolate (5-MTHF) 400–800 mcg daily** — MTHFR A1298C +/+ combined with MTHFD1 and MTRR means your entire methylation system is running at reduced capacity. Never take regular folic acid.
2. **Take methylcobalamin (B12) 1,000 mcg daily** — MTRR (+/+) impairs B12 recycling in the methylation cycle. B12 and methylfolate must be taken together.
3. **Get blood labs: homocysteine, thyroid panel, TPO/TGAb antibodies, Vitamin D, hsCRP** — Your autoimmune and thyroid variants demand monitored targets.
4. **Eliminate seed oils completely** — PON1 (+/+) means oxidized fats from canola, soy, and sunflower oils cause direct arterial and cellular damage.
5. **Protect your serotonin system** — HTR2A C/C is your most significant mental health variant. Sleep 8 hours, get morning sunlight, limit caffeine and alcohol, and consider magnesium + 5-HTP at night.
