## What to Avoid in Supplements

| Avoid | Reason |
|-------|--------|
| **Folic acid** (synthetic B9) | MTHFR A1298C cannot process; blocks methylfolate |
| **Cyanocobalamin (B12)** | Requires methylation for conversion; use methylcobalamin |
| **High-dose iodine (> 500 mcg)** | Can trigger autoimmune thyroid flares (HLA risk) |
| **Iron** (unless ferritin confirmed low) | No hemochromatosis risk but iron supplements not needed unless labs show deficiency |
| **St. John's Wort** | Serotonin syndrome risk with HTR2A hypersensitivity; avoid unless medically supervised |
