## Absolute Avoidances (Zero Tolerance)

### Seed Oils — CDKAL1 Gut + Autoimmune + General Health

> Note: Unlike Sergio, your PON1 is favorable (Leu/Leu), so this isn't driven by a PON1 defect. But seed oils oxidize easily and feed inflammation — directly relevant to your CDKAL1 gut and HLA/STAT4 autoimmune risk — so eliminating them is still strongly recommended.

| ❌ Avoid | Common Hiding Places |
|----------|---------------------|
| Canola oil | "Vegetable oil," restaurant cooking, most salad dressings |
| Soybean oil | Mayonnaise, margarine, most processed foods |
| Sunflower oil | Chips, crackers, granola bars |
| Corn oil | Popcorn, processed snacks |
| Cottonseed oil | Fast food fryers |
| Grapeseed oil | "Healthy" restaurant options |
| "Vegetable oil" (any blend) | Virtually everywhere in packaged food |

**Alternatives:** Extra virgin olive oil (cold use + light heat), avocado oil (high heat), ghee or tallow (high heat), coconut oil.

**Restaurant rule:** Ask what oil they cook in. Request olive oil, butter, or ghee when available. Avoid anything deep-fried unless you can confirm the oil.

## Avoid / Minimize (Significant Impact)

### Alcohol — MTHFR · COMT · HTR2A · CDKAL1

- Alcohol depletes B vitamins required for your methylation pathway (MTHFR + MTRR)
- Alcohol causes serotonin "rebound depletion" after the initial surge — HTR2A C/C makes this more pronounced (worse mood hangover even with small amounts)
- Worsens COMT estrogen metabolism → estrogen clearance slows
- Disrupts gut microbiome integrity (CDKAL1 IBD risk)
- **If drinking:** Red wine in moderation (resveratrol partially compensates); never on empty stomach; take B vitamins day before and after
