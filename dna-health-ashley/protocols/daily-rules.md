## Morning (Wake → Noon)

### Non-Negotiables
- [ ] **Get 15–20 min morning sunlight within 1 hour of waking** — HTR2A C/C makes this your most powerful daily mood and serotonin intervention; also triggers vitamin D synthesis (HLA autoimmune support)
- [ ] **Take morning supplements** (see [Supplement Stack](supplements.md)):
  - 5-MTHF (methylfolate) 400–800 mcg — **critical for MTHFR A1298C + MTHFD1**
  - Methylcobalamin B12 1,000 mcg sublingual — **critical for MTRR +/+**
  - Vitamin D3 3,000–5,000 IU + K2
  - Betaine (TMG) 500 mg
  - P5P (active B6) 25 mg
  - Riboflavin (B2) 25 mg
- [ ] **No folic acid** — check any supplement labels; avoid products with "folic acid" listed
- [ ] **Protein with breakfast** — eggs (daily; choline is a direct methyl donor for your COMT/MTHFR)

### Coffee Rule
- 1–2 cups maximum before 11 AM
- Nothing caffeinated after noon — ADORA2A C/C (rs2298383) suggests caffeine sensitivity; HTR2A hypersensitivity amplifies caffeine-driven anxiety

## Midday (12 PM → 6 PM)

- [ ] **Eat berries with or after lunch** — ½–1 cup blueberries/raspberries (CDKAL1 gut + cardiovascular + brain protection)
- [ ] **Seed oils: zero tolerance** — they feed CDKAL1 gut + HLA/STAT4 autoimmune inflammation; ask about cooking oil at restaurants
- [ ] **Cruciferous vegetable at lunch or dinner** — broccoli, cauliflower, kale, Brussels sprouts (GSTP1 upregulation + DIM for estrogen clearance + COMT support)
- [ ] **No sitting > 45 minutes** without a 5–10 min movement break — HLA + IL2RA joint inflammation risk

### Movement
- [ ] Exercise at least 4x/week (see [Exercise](exercise.md)) — most impactful intervention for HTR2A anxiety/mood AND HLA joint health AND COMT estrogen metabolism

## Evening (6 PM → Sleep)

### Dinner
- [ ] **Include omega-3 source** — fatty fish 3–4x/week (HLA anti-inflammation, DIO2 thyroid, HTR2A serotonin membrane support)
- [ ] **Seafood 2–3x/week** for selenium (DIO2 thyroid + thyroid antibody reduction) and iodine
- [ ] **Fermented food** — goat yogurt, kefir, kimchi, sauerkraut (you CAN have dairy — MCM6 A/G = lactase persistent)

### Evening Supplements
- [ ] **Magnesium glycinate 300–400 mg** — HTR2A anxiety, sleep quality, COMT methylation support
- [ ] **5-HTP 50–100 mg (optional but recommended)** — HTR2A hypersensitivity makes this particularly effective for sleep and mood; do not combine with SSRIs
- [ ] **DIM 100–200 mg** (with dinner) — COMT estrogen metabolism support

### Sleep Hygiene (Non-Negotiable for HTR2A)
- [ ] **8 hours in bed — strict target** — sleep deprivation is the fastest way to crash your serotonin system; for you, this is medical
- [ ] **Blue light off 90 min before sleep** — melatonin production and serotonin recycling both impacted
- [ ] **No alcohol within 3 hours of sleep** — HTR2A makes serotonin rebound from alcohol particularly disruptive to sleep quality
- [ ] **Room temperature 65–68°F (18–20°C)** — deep sleep maximizes brain cleanup (CLU T/C partial protection)
- [ ] **No D3 in the evening** — inhibits melatonin
