## Why This Matters

**LPA clear:** Unlike Sergio, you have NO genetic elevation of Lipoprotein(a). Lp(a) is genetically set and one of the most impactful cardiovascular risk markers. You don't have this burden.

**Factor V Leiden and Prothrombin — especially important for women:**
- Factor V Leiden (rs6025) is present in ~5% of people of European ancestry and increases DVT/PE risk 4–7x (heterozygous) to 80x (homozygous)
- Prothrombin G20210A increases clotting risk ~3x
- **You carry neither.** This is highly significant if you use oral contraceptives, plan pregnancies, or are ever prescribed HRT. You have no genetic clotting barrier to any of these.

**No hemochromatosis:** Your HFE gene is clear. You have normal iron metabolism — no risk of progressive iron overload that can silently damage liver, heart, and joints.

**CTH clear:** Your transsulfuration pathway (which clears homocysteine → cysteine → glutathione) is genetically intact. Unlike Sergio, your homocysteine is not doubly blocked.

## What This Means for Your Protocol
