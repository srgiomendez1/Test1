## Protocol

- Anti-inflammatory diet (same as HLA file)
- **Boswellia (Boswellic acids) 300–500 mg** — directly inhibits 5-LOX (leukotriene) pathway; clinically studied for joint inflammation; 12-week trials show comparable effects to NSAIDs without GI risk
- **Glucosamine sulfate 1,500 mg/day** — joint structural support; 3-year RCT data for cartilage preservation
- **Omega-3 3–4 g/day** — suppresses inflammatory cytokines (IL-1β, TNF-α, IL-6) that drive joint breakdown
- Movement: daily joint mobility circuit (10 min), low-impact cardio, no sitting > 45 min
- **Avoid NSAIDs routinely** — your CDKAL1 makes gut damage from NSAIDs more likely; use only for acute pain

Monitor: Any symmetric joint swelling, morning stiffness > 30 min → see HLA-STAT4 file and get tested.
