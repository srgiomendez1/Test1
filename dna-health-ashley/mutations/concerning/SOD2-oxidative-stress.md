## What SOD2 Does

SOD2 encodes manganese superoxide dismutase (MnSOD), the mitochondria's front-line antioxidant. It neutralizes superoxide radicals produced during normal energy (ATP) generation. The Val16 allele disrupts the mitochondrial targeting sequence → slightly less efficient import → mildly reduced SOD2 activity.

## Practical Implications (Mild)

- Slightly higher baseline mitochondrial oxidative stress
- Modest reduction in cellular energy efficiency under high metabolic load
- Note: This is a background vulnerability, not a primary clinical risk in heterozygous form
