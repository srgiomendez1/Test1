## What This Means

HLA (Human Leukocyte Antigen) genes control how the immune system distinguishes self from non-self. These variants — all in the HLA region on chromosome 6 — increase the probability that the immune system **misfires and attacks joint tissues**. Combined:
- HLA-DRB1 alone: 4x RA risk above baseline
- Combined with DRB9 and DQA1: cumulative risk is substantial
- **Critical:** These variants are *inflammation and hyperglycemia-dependent*. Keeping blood sugar below 85 mg/dL and inflammation low can effectively suppress expression of these risks

## Protocol

**Blood sugar is the master lever:**
- Fasted glucose: Target **< 85 mg/dL** (< 4.7 mmol/L)
- HbA1c: < 5.4%
- If you have chronic joint pain: try keto or carnivore diet for 6–8 weeks and observe

**Anti-inflammatory foundation:**
- Omega-3s (EPA+DHA) — 2–4 g/day: directly reduces inflammatory cytokines
- Turmeric/curcumin — 500 mg standardized extract; inhibits NF-kB inflammatory pathway
- Eliminate cow dairy (MCM6 variant) and seed oils (PON1 variant) — both directly increase the inflammation that triggers HLA joint attacks

**Exercise:**
- Joint mobility work daily (15–20 min) — stiffness and lack of movement triggers inflammatory cascades in genetically susceptible individuals
- Avoid extended sedentary periods — your ITGAV bloodflow variant makes this especially important

**Monitoring:**
- If joint pain develops: get rheumatoid factor (RF) and anti-CCP antibodies tested
- Early treatment prevents joint destruction — do not ignore persistent joint symptoms
