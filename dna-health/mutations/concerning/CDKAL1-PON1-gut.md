## What This Means

### CDKAL1 (rs6908425) — Leaky Gut / GI Distress
CDKAL1 encodes CDK5 regulatory subunit associated protein 1-like 1, involved in intestinal cell integrity and gut microbiome health. The C/C variant is associated with:
- **Increased intestinal permeability** ("leaky gut")
- Higher susceptibility to GI inflammation
- Connection to your serotonin variants (90% of serotonin made in gut)
- Connection to your thyroid variants (leaky gut → molecular mimicry → Hashimoto's attacks)
- Connection to your HLA RA variants (leaky gut → immune dysregulation → joint attacks)

This is a foundational variant — fixing gut health improves nearly every other risk in your profile.

### PON1 (rs854560) — Seed Oil Sensitivity
Paraoxonase 1 (PON1) is an antioxidant enzyme carried on HDL cholesterol that neutralizes oxidized lipids. The A/A (Leu55Met) variant significantly reduces PON1 activity, meaning:
- Oxidized fats from seed oils are not neutralized and remain in circulation
- These oxidized lipids directly damage arterial walls (compounds LPA risk)
- They damage gut lining (compounds CDKAL1 leaky gut risk)
- They drive systemic inflammation (compounds every inflammatory variant you carry)

## Protocol

### Heal the Gut (Priority)
**Eliminate gut irritants completely:**
- Seed oils (canola, soybean, sunflower, corn, cottonseed, grapeseed oil) — PON1 makes these especially harmful
- Cow dairy (MCM6 +/+)
- Excess alcohol
- NSAIDs (ibuprofen, naproxen) when avoidable — directly increase intestinal permeability
- Gluten (test sensitivity; highly common with CDKAL1 variants)

**Eat gut-healing foods:**
- **Berries (daily)** — explicitly flagged as PRIORITY for CDKAL1; flavonoids heal gut lining
- Bone broth — collagen, glycine support gut integrity
- Fermented foods: goat yogurt, kimchi, sauerkraut, kombucha
- Fatty fish: omega-3 DHA/EPA reduce gut inflammation

**Supplements for gut healing:**
- **Zinc carnosine** — 75–150 mg/day; most evidence-backed supplement for gut barrier integrity
- **L-Glutamine** — 5–10 g/day; primary fuel for intestinal cells
- **Probiotic** — multi-strain, 50+ billion CFU; rotate strains every 2 months
- **Digestive enzymes** — with main meals; supports complete digestion and reduces undigested protein reaching the colon

### Seed Oils — Zero Tolerance
Replace with:
| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use Instead |
|----------|---------------|
| Canola oil | Extra virgin olive oil |
| Soybean oil | Avocado oil (for high-heat) |
| Sunflower oil | Ghee or tallow (for high-heat) |
| Corn oil | Coconut oil |
| Vegetable oil (any blend) | Butter (grass-fed, if tolerated) |

**Read restaurant menus carefully** — most restaurants cook with seed oils. Ask what oil is used; request cooking in olive oil or butter.
