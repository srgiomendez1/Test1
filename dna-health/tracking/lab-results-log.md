# Lab Results Log

> Log all results here with date. Track trends over time to measure impact of interventions.

---

## How to Use This Log

1. After each blood test, add a new row to the relevant table
2. Note any supplements or protocol changes in the "Notes" column
3. Compare to targets listed in [Lab Tests Guide](../protocols/lab-tests.md)
