## Your Genetic Profile at a Glance

### The Core Story
Your DNA tells a clear story: you have a **methylation problem** (MTHFR + MTHFD1), a **cardiovascular elevation risk** (LPA + CTH + homocysteine), a **thyroid vulnerability** (TG gene), and **poor detox capacity** (GSTP1). Fortunately, most of these have specific, well-studied interventions. Your endurance genes (ACTN3, NRF2) and fasting-response genes (ADIPOQ) are strong assets.

## The 5 Non-Negotiables (Start Here)

1. **Take methylfolate (5-MTHF) 400–800 mcg daily** — MTHFR C677T +/+ is your most critical finding. Never take regular folic acid.
2. **Get blood labs: Lp(a), homocysteine, Vitamin D, ferritin, testosterone, thyroid panel** — Multiple variants demand monitored targets.
3. **Eliminate cow dairy entirely** — MCM6 +/+ means cow dairy triggers systemic inflammation in you.
4. **Eliminate seed oils completely** — PON1/GSTP1 variants make oxidized fats acutely damaging.
5. **Reduce inflammation aggressively** — Your thyroid, joint, and cardiovascular genes are all *inflammation-triggered*.
